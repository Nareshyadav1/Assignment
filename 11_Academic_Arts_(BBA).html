Bachelors of Business Administration (BBA)

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is a bachelor's degree in business administration. In the United States, the degree is conferred after four years of full-time study in one or more areas of business concentrations. In Europe, the degree is conferred after three years of full-time study in one or more areas of business concentrations. The BBA program usually includes general business courses and advanced courses for specific concentrations.
